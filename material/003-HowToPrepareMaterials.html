How to prepare the materials?

There are two ways to prepare materials:

  1. Write a second, greatly simplified App (Presentation App),
  2. Prepare examples for each required feature.

It might be easier to prepare standalone examples (but they have to be minimal), but Presentation App shows natural cycle of development and also keeps consistency between slides.

Remember that the slides should also be runnable; don't present snippets - you cannot play around with them or get the full picture.
