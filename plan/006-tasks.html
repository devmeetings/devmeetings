Sprint 1 - The App

Basic

  1. Prepare a right name for you workshop. Think about the target audience.
  2. Write down the expected end result and a list of relevant features of framework / language.
  3. Prepare the Backlog (a list of features of the App that participants will write).

Additional

  1. Divide the Backlog into Sprints. What will participants do during the first 20 minutes of Devmeeting?
  2. Write the App on your own; keep it minimal, recall what mistakes you made when you were a rookie.

Extra

  1. Comment important lines when you are writing the app.
  2. Prepare commits with proper granularity - each commit shouldn't have more than 5-20 lines of code.
