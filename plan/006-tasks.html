Iteration 1 - The App

  1. Do you have an app that your language / framework is most suitable for?
  2. What is the minimal version of this app? What you would do in 5 minutes?
  3. Try to write the app on your own; keep it minimal. Define tasks after that.
  4. Recall what mistakes you made when you were starting to learn the language / framework.
  5. Think of second app you will use to present your language / framework of choice.
